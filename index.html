Kerala "God's Own Country" is the perfect introduction to travel in India. It plays a major role in India tourism and deserves the attentions of tourists from all over the world.

There are a lot of tourism options in this culturally and geographically rich state of Kerala, India.

During your Kerala tour you will have wonderful opportunity to explore serene backwaters and rolling hills, gushing waterfalls and adrenaline-pumping boat races; ancient culture in modern cities; delectable cuisine and colorful festivals
